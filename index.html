1. Children’s Privacy

PixelLearn is designed for children. We take children’s privacy seriously and aim to collect as little information as possible.

2. Information We Collect

PixelLearn does not require users to create an account or provide personal information such as a real name, email address, phone number, home address, or precise location.

The app only stores:

  • game level progress
  • a randomly generated user name or identifier created by the app for gameplay purposes

This random name or identifier is not intended to identify the child in real life.

3. How We Use Information

We use this limited information only to save the user’s game progress, restore progress when the app is used again, and support basic app functionality.

4. Firebase

PixelLearn uses Firebase to store game progress data.

This may include level progress, a random in-app name or random identifier, and technical data necessary to provide Firebase functionality.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We only use Firebase as a service provider to store and support game progress data.

6. Data Retention

Game progress and the random in-app identifier may be stored for as long as needed to provide app functionality.

7. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect the limited data used by the app. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

8. Parents’ Rights

Parents or guardians who have questions about the app’s data practices may contact us using the email below.
